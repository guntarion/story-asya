# Metal Cardbot Season 1 - Character Guide

> Reference guide for writing stories based on Metal Cardbot Season 1 characters.
> Source: [Metal Cardbot Wiki (Fandom)](https://metal-cardbot.fandom.com/wiki/Category:S1_Cardbots)

---

## World Overview

The Metal Cardbots are mechanical lifeforms from Planet Machina, a world governed by the supercomputer **Deus Machina**. When Machina was destroyed, the Cardbots fled in escape pods and arrived on Earth, adopting vehicle forms to blend in with humanity. The series is set in **Mowtown**, where human partner **Jun Grant** uses the **Metal Breath** (a wrist-mounted device) to command Cardbots in battle by slotting **Metal Cards** that summon their weapons.

### Key Concepts
- **Machinasium**: Living metal that forms Cardbot bodies. They bleed light-green fluid when injured.
- **Metal Breath**: Wrist device given by Deus Machina to Blue Cop, who passed it to Jun. Used to seal/deploy Cardbots and summon their weapons.
- **Metal Cards**: Storage media containing weapons. Each Cardbot has a unique Metal Card weapon.
- **Sealing**: Defeated or willing Cardbots can be stored as energy inside a Metal Card.
- **Star Guardians**: Elite protectors of Deus Machina on Planet Machina.
- **Edo Motors**: Edo's garage in Mowtown, the Cardbots' base of operations on Earth.

## Human Characters

### Jun Grant (준 그랜트) - The Protagonist
- **Role**: Primary wielder of the Metal Breath; field commander of the Cardbots
- **Personality**: Energetic, brave, strong sense of justice. Starts impulsive but matures into a tactician
- **Visual**: Red hoodie and blue jacket (S1)
- **Key Trait**: Makes real-time strategic decisions about which Cardbot to deploy and which weapon card to slot
- **Voice Actor (KR)**: Jang Ye-na

### Theo (테오) - The Analyst
- **Role**: Jun's best friend; provides scientific analysis and intel
- **Personality**: Intellectual, loyal, often discovers enemy weaknesses
- **Visual**: Glasses-wearing "smart kid" archetype

### Anna (안나) - The Mediator
- **Role**: Daughter of Mayor Jack; bridges authorities and Cardbots
- **Personality**: High emotional intelligence, voice of reason, often recognizes Cardbot emotions first
- **Visual**: Pink dress shirt and skirt (S1)

### Edo (에도) - The Mechanic
- **Role**: Runs Edo Motors garage; Cardbots' doctor/mechanic on Earth
- **Personality**: Initially hostile to Cardbots (property damage), later accepts them. Financially struggles from their energy costs
- **Key Trait**: Bridges human engineering and alien biology

### Crest (크래스트) - The Rival Partner
- **Role**: Wild Guardy's human partner; wealthy, aloof rival to Jun
- **Personality**: Refined, initially secretive, eventually becomes a strong ally
- **Key Trait**: Twin brother of Soleil (Season 3 character)

## Season 1 Cardbots - Protagonists/Allies

### 1. Blue Cop (블루캅) - The Lawman

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Korean** | 블루캅 (Beullu Kab) |
| **Affiliation** | Star Guardians / Edo Motors |
| **Vehicle Mode** | Lamborghini Gallardo LP560-4 Police Car |
| **Metal Card** | Justice Gauntlet |
| **Color** | Blue |
| **Voice Actor (KR)** | Kwak Yoon-sang (곽윤상) |

**Role**: Secondary protagonist. The narrative center of the Cardbot faction.

**Background**: A former Star Guardian on Planet Machina, tasked with protecting Deus Machina. Before Machina's destruction, Deus Machina gave him the Metal Breath with the instruction: "use the Metal Breath to find a friend." He arrived on Earth, adopted a police car disguise, and studied human customs. He entrusted the Metal Breath to Jun Grant after it chose him.

**Personality**: Thoughtful and caring but acts cold at times. Stoic, serious, and initially inflexible. Studied human culture in-depth (knows how to make coffee, give massages). Can be clumsy. Relies on Jun to command his actions in battle. His arc involves learning that justice requires empathy, not just rules.

**Appearance**: Blue Cardbot based on a police officer with a detachable police hat bearing the Star Guardian insignia, a bullet symbol with flap extensions on each side, light-blue eyes, magenta visor, white and blue chest, large car fenders as shoulders.

**Quote**: *"We've lost our home, and now we have to survive on this planet. Together with Earthlings, like that boy!"*

**Arsenal & Techniques**:
- **Justice Gauntlet**: Large translucent blue gauntlet on right arm with double-barreled gun
- **Thunderbolt Smash**: Gathers electricity in gauntlet, jumps and punches
- **Burst Shot**: Fires single or consecutive shots from gauntlet gun
- **Mega Volt Punch**: Main ultimate - gathers electricity for a powerful energy punch
- **Infinity Force Break**: Advanced ultimate - unites Jun's mind and body to generate and launch multiple energy punches
- **Energy Bullet Shooter**: Blaster port on each forearm

**Relationships**: Best friends with Jun Grant. Respected ally of Mega Trucker. Rival relationship with Wild Guardy (who idolized him). Former enemy of Heavy Iron and Black Hook.

**Story Notes**: Blue Cop is the only Cardbot to appear in all seasons. His original name was "Silver Bullet." He was the first Metal Cardbot ever designed.

### 2. Mega Trucker (메가트러커) - The Dancing Tank

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Korean** | 메가트러커 (Mega Teuleokeo) |
| **Affiliation** | Edo Motors |
| **Vehicle Mode** | Dump Truck (based on Isuzu Giga) |
| **Metal Card** | Mega Protector |
| **Color** | Orange |
| **Voice Actor (KR)** | Hong Beom-gi (홍범기) |

**Role**: The team's tank/defender. First Cardbot sealed by Jun.

**Background**: After landing on Earth, Mega Trucker immediately began seeking out music venues and dancing in the open, against Blue Cop's wishes. His antics at Edo Motors caused the Metal Breath to end up with Jun Grant. He was lured back and became the first Cardbot Jun sealed.

**Personality**: Carefree, doesn't bother blending in. Loves dancing, with his favorite genre being Latin music. Defies the "dumb brute" stereotype - he moves with rhythmic grace that belies his bulk. Hates complexity, prefers straightforward, honest interactions.

**Appearance**: Orange Cardbot based on an American football player with light-blue eyes, football helmet-based head with fist symbol on each side, yellow-orange sections on limbs and head, white hands.

**Quote**: *"I'm going to live my life dancing the way my heart wants!"*

**Arsenal & Techniques**:
- **Mega Protector**: Translucent orange shield with saw blade, mounted on left arm
- **Mega Power Tackle**: Main ultimate - delivers powerful tackle with the Mega Protector
- **Shoulder Shield**: Deploys energy shield around body for protection
- **Mega Touchdown**: Slams opponent with Mega Protector while jumping
- **Ultra Mega Power Shield**: Advanced technique (Edo's modification) - combines power of other Cardbots into a giant defensive shield strong enough to block falling escape pods

**Relationships**: Close ally of Blue Cop. Strength rivals Buffalo Crush. One of Jun's secondary default Cardbots alongside Shadow X.

**Story Notes**: Original name was "Motorgrader." Shares Blue Cop's role as "primary punching bag" in combat due to his frontline position.

### 3. Mega Ambler (메가앰블러) - The Shadow Doctor

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Korean** | 메가앰블러 (Mega Aembeulleo) |
| **Affiliation** | Edo Motors |
| **Vehicle Mode** | FMTV Military Ambulance |
| **Metal Card** | Mega Forceps |
| **Color** | White |
| **Voice Actor (KR)** | Seo Won-seok (서원석) |

**Role**: Team medic and support unit.

**Background**: On Machina, he was famous for his medical prowess but infamous for being unlicensed and charging exorbitant prices. On Earth, he became the urban legend "The Black Ambulance" - rolling objects at humans to knock them out, then "treating" them in a warehouse. After being defeated by Blue Cop and sealed by Jun, he was cleaned up and became the Cardbots' official medic.

**Personality**: Morally grey but skilled. His darker introduction sets him apart. Eventually aligns with Jun and makes peace with the team. Initially grimy/dirty on arrival.

**Appearance**: White Cardbot with green and red colors throughout, light-blue left eye, yellow scope on right eye for analyzing subjects, green medical cross symbol on each side of head, gauntlets that form vehicle's under grill.

**Quote**: *"But you can create a weakness!"*

**Arsenal & Techniques**:
- **Mega Forceps**: Translucent white medical forceps gauntlet on right arm
- **Forceps Crush**: Grabs enemy's weak point to restrict movement
- **Forceps Shock**: Main ultimate - paralyzes enemies with powerful electric shock via forceps
- **Mega Bandage**: White bandage rolls for medical aid or binding opponents
- **Recycle Shot**: Green energy beam for temporary Cardbot repairs; can form healing dome for multiple targets
- **Sanitizer Spray**: Green sanitizing mist from right index finger

**Relationships**: Made peace with Theo after their initially rough introduction. Essential support for the entire team.

**Story Notes**: Original name was "Doctor Storm." Was originally meant to wield a sniper rifle - bullet-shaped plates on thighs are remnants of this concept.

### 4. Shadow X (쉐도우X) - The Aerial Ninja

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Korean** | 쉐도우X (Swedou Egseu) |
| **Affiliation** | Edo Motors |
| **Vehicle Mode** | VTOL Aircraft (jet with shark jaw painting) |
| **Metal Card** | X-Wheel |
| **Color** | Violet |
| **Voice Actor (KR)** | Kim Myung-jun (김명준) |

**Role**: Stealth and aerial specialist. The smallest, lightest, and youngest Cardbot.

**Background**: A professional spy who initially believed Jun was using the Metal Breath to enslave Cardbots. He harassed Jun using stealth mode until Jun figured out he could spot Shadow X's shadow. After Jun earned his respect by defeating the Heli-Tornado, Shadow X graciously allowed himself to be sealed.

**Personality**: Immature and mischievous like a child, but determined in battle and fiercely protective of those he acknowledges. Initially distrustful of humans.

**Appearance**: Violet Cardbot designed after a Japanese shinobi with white forehead protector sporting a red "X," red shuriken symbol on each side of head, green eyes, wide forearms to store yellow three-blade rotors.

**Quote**: *"I refuse to submit to a brat like this!"*

**Arsenal & Techniques**:
- **X-Wheel**: Translucent violet Fuma Shuriken mounted on either arm. Can also be ridden for flight. Usable for close-range and long-range attacks/defense
- **Spinning Cutter**: Throws energy shuriken with the X-Wheel; can throw continuously or attack from behind
- **Heli-Tornado**: Main ultimate - spins X-Wheel to create a powerful whirlwind
- **X-Launcher**: Rocket launcher on each shoulder; fires violet energy beams
- **Stealth Mode**: Camouflage for either form. Only applies transparency - cannot hide sounds or shadows

**Relationships**: One of Jun's secondary default Cardbots alongside Mega Trucker. Shares Blue Cop's "punching bag" role in combat.

**Story Notes**: Original name was "Shadow Gale." First flying Metal Cardbot to appear.

### 5. Dexter (덱스터) - The Eco-Warrior

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Korean** | 덱스터 (Degseuteo) |
| **Affiliation** | Edo Motors |
| **Vehicle Mode** | Excavator (four wheels, not treads) |
| **Metal Card** | Dexter Drill |
| **Color** | Yellow |
| **Voice Actor (KR)** | Shin Yong-woo (신용우) |

**Role**: Subterranean combat specialist.

**Background**: After landing on Earth, Dexter skulked around Mowtown scaring humans and destroying construction equipment, mistaking construction sites as attacks on nature. He was commonly mistaken for a dragon due to fog. Wild Guardy damaged his eyes during a clash, forcing him to fight blind underground using hearing alone - causing mass havoc. Jun deployed Phoenix Fire to overwhelm his hearing underground, then sealed him. Edo later repaired his eyesight.

**Personality**: Deeply devoted to nature and life. Merciless toward humans who destroy nature until joining Jun and learning that humans respect nature too. Became more peaceful after the team showed him natural areas in Mowtown.

**Appearance**: Yellow Cardbot with light-blue eyes, hard hat-based head with silver screw cap symbol on each side, binocular-based lenses on forehead, heavy armored body with excavator arm on back as a tail, black plow shovel on posterior.

**Quote**: *"Do you know how many forest lives are sacrificed because of humans?!?!"*

**Arsenal & Techniques**:
- **Dexter Drill**: Translucent yellow drill gauntlet on either arm
- **Spear Thrust**: Stabs opponent with spiraling drill
- **Spiral Ray**: Main ultimate - fires powerful beam from drill
- **Helix Sword**: Gathers energy into spiraling drill, transforms into energy lance
- **Excavator Arm**: Vehicle form's shovel arm placed on posterior as tail

**Relationships**: Initially hostile to all humans. Fought Wild Guardy early on.

**Story Notes**: Director wanted to convey his elongated excavator head in robot form. The toy has drill on left arm, animation shows right arm - common mecha discrepancy.

### 6. Phoenix Fire (피닉스파이어) - The First Responder

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Korean** | 피닉스파이어 (Pinigseu Paieo) |
| **Affiliation** | Unnamed Firefighter Group / Edo Motors |
| **Vehicle Mode** | Fire Engine (Hyundai Mega Truck base) |
| **Metal Card** | Fire Hammer |
| **Color** | Red |
| **Voice Actor (KR)** | Hwang Chang-young (황창영) |

**Role**: Combines combat with disaster relief. Represents the "Rescue" aspect.

**Background**: A renowned firefighter on Machina who won a medal for bravery. When his escape pod caught fire entering Earth's atmosphere, he developed severe pyrophobia (fear of fire). On Earth, he compulsively extinguished ANY fire - barbecue grills, birthday candles, even classroom demonstration candles. Jun staged a fire alarm to clear an area, then Blue Cop's burst shots (flaming projectiles) triggered his fear, allowing Jun to seal him. The team later helped him overcome his phobia.

**Personality**: Brave at heart but traumatized. His phobia made him act irrationally (breaking through windows to extinguish birthday candles). With the team's support, he overcame his fear.

**Appearance**: Red Cardbot designed after a Japanese fire brigade uniform with light-blue eyes, Kajikabuto & Sashiko cloth combo helmet-shaped head with flame symbol on each side, large shoulders with ladder adornment, dark-grey forearms with rear bumper adornment, hazard stripe pattern on shins.

**Quote**: *"Do you know how scary a fire is? Fire must be put out immediately!"*

**Arsenal & Techniques**:
- **Fire Hammer**: Translucent red hammer gauntlet shield on left arm
- **Burning Hammer**: Main ultimate - generates and gathers fire into hammer, then unleashes powerful flaming strike
- **Hammer Quake**: Strikes ground with hammer to create shockwave; absorbing fire increases power
- **Aqua Cannon**: Giant gun transforming from vehicle's deck gun; fires high-pressure water
- **Water Splash**: Fires powerful water stream; can extinguish fires, push/reflect opponents, or boost jumping height

**Relationships**: Closely allied with the rescue/support role alongside Mega Ambler.

**Story Notes**: Original name was "Firebuster." The deck gun is mounted off-center left for symmetrical robot shoulders.

### 7. Wild Guardy (와일드가디) - The Rival Knight

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Korean** | 와일드가디 (Waildeu Gadi) |
| **Affiliation** | Edo Motors |
| **Vehicle Mode** | Sports Car (Ford Mustang) |
| **Metal Card** | Wild Shield + Wild Calibur |
| **Color** | Silver / Violet-Blue |
| **Voice Actor (KR)** | Sang-Hyeon Eom (엄상현) |

**Role**: Rival to Blue Cop. Crest's partner.

**Background**: On Machina, Wild Guardy idolized Blue Cop as the greatest Star Guardian and dreamed of becoming one. Machina was destroyed before he had the chance. He crash-landed on Earth with terrible damage, and Crest found and repaired him. After recovering, his goal shifted to besting Blue Cop and becoming king of all Metal Cardbots. First appeared saving Jun's group from Heavy Iron with a surprise slash. Eventually challenged Blue Cop to a final duel and was defeated, after which Crest allowed Jun to seal him.

**Personality**: Arrogant, competitive, obsessed with proving superiority over Blue Cop. His relationship with Crest is built on mutual elitism, though they eventually humble themselves. A European knight motif.

**Appearance**: Violet-blue Cardbot with knight design, gladiator helmet-shaped head with gold wing on each side, yellow eyes, gold sections on torso, silver sections on outer shoulders and forearms.

**Quote**: *"My name is Wild Guardy. I will be the king of the Metal Cardbots."*

**Arsenal & Techniques**:
- **Wild Shield**: Purple translucent shield; doubles as scabbard for the Wild Calibur; combines with sword to form a greatsword. Capable of offense and defense
- **Wild Calibur**: Translucent purple sword stored in the Wild Shield
- **Wild Slash**: Main ultimate - gathers energy into Wild Calibur for a powerful sword slash
- **Wild Rave**: Powerful charging slash attack
- **Guardian Shield**: Emerald translucent shield created by Buster Gallon; stronger defense than Wild Shield but lacks sword capability

**Relationships**: Partner of Crest. Rival of Blue Cop (whom he once idolized). Initially hostile but becomes a trusted ally.

**Story Notes**: Original name was "Gold Hunter." Originally light blue and orange colored.

### 8. Fleta Z (플레타Z) - The Sky Hunter

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Korean** | 플레타Z (Peulleta Jedeu) |
| **Affiliation** | Edo Motors |
| **Vehicle Mode** | Transport Aircraft (based on An-124) |
| **Metal Card** | Fleta Bow |
| **Color** | Black |
| **Voice Actor (KR)** | Ryu Seung-gon (류승곤) |

**Role**: Long-range aerial combat specialist. Hunter.

**Background**: On Machina, Fleta Z was a skilled hunter who lived on the outskirts of society - his existence was merely a rumor. His pod crash-landed on a small tropical island where he lived in solitary until a tourist dropped their phone. Through it, he learned about Earth and other Metal Cardbots. He contacted the video uploader hoping to meet other Cardbots but was hostile to Jun and Blue Cop because they used the Metal Breath to control others. Easily blew off Jun's Cardbots until Wild Guardy intervened. Eventually sealed and immediately made peace with the group.

**Personality**: Self-proclaimed "free spirit." Independent, values freedom above all. Dreams of living without being bound by anything. Confident in his own strength.

**Appearance**: Black Cardbot with green eyes, eagle head-design helmet with flap and turbine symbol on each side, red and golden-orange patterns on shoulders, red chest with bird symbol, large plane wings that fold inward when not in use.

**Quote**: *"The thing is, I'm strong without that."*

**Arsenal & Techniques**:
- **Fleta Bow**: Translucent sea-green archer's bow that fires laser arrows. Transforms into a wrist blade for close combat
- **Beak Arrow**: Creates and fires energy arrows - essentially infinite ammo since arrows are generated from energy
- **Bird Flock Shot**: Fires multiple energy arrows with time-lapse or rain-like shooting patterns
- **Vulture Wings**: Ultimate technique - fires a giant energy arrow with the silhouette of a bird of prey

**Relationships**: Last ally Cardbot sealed before the antagonist arc. Initially hostile to Jun but quickly made peace after being defeated.

**Story Notes**: Original name was "Sonic One" (white and light-blue colored). The toy has a roof-portion sword that was never used in the show.

## Season 1 Cardbots - Antagonists

### 9. Black Hook (블랙후크) - The Pirate Captain

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Korean** | 블랙후크 (Beullaeg Hukeu) |
| **Affiliation** | Black Hook Crew (Captain) / Edo Motors (later) |
| **Vehicle Mode** | Pirate Galleon (with flight capability) |
| **Metal Card** | Captain Cannon |
| **Color** | Navy-Blue |
| **Voice Actor (KR)** | Lee Hyun (이현) |

**Role**: Secondary antagonist. Captain of the Black Hook Pirates (Black Hook, Buffalo Crush, Buster Gallon).

**Background**: A notorious pirate on Machina who bested many Star Guardians. Friend of Buffalo Crush, who was a member of his crew. Arrived on Earth with the treasure of the Star Crown, which Heavy Iron later stole from him. All three crew members were eventually sealed by Jun by Episode 24. Later reforms and joins Jun's group, though he and Heavy Iron still butt heads frequently.

**Personality**: Commanding, aggressive, prideful. A pirate through and through. Eventually reforms but maintains a sour rivalry with Heavy Iron.

**Appearance**: Navy-blue Cardbot with pirate captain motif: pirate hat-style head, yellow eyes, skull symbol on each side, red eyepatch (removable for targeting scope), broad shoulders and thorax reminiscent of 18th century royal navy jacket, chipped sections on face/chest/hands. Second tallest Cardbot after Heavy Iron.

**Quote**: *"I will definitely get my hands on the legacy of Deus Machina!"*

**Arsenal & Techniques**:
- **Captain Cannon**: Translucent black cannon on right shoulder
- **Captain's Cannon**: Main ultimate - fires cannonball-shaped energy bullet
- **Grand Captain's Cannon**: Enhanced version - absorbs electric energy or ally power for huge energy bullet
- **Black Cannon**: Vehicle mode cannons; fires while flying
- **Broadside Shot**: Pair of two-barreled cannons on back
- **Captain Anchor**: Anchor-shaped melee weapon

**Relationships**: Captain of Buffalo Crush and Buster Gallon. Frequent rival of Heavy Iron. Eventually allies with Jun.

### 10. Buffalo Crush (버팔로크러쉬) - The Viking Bruiser

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Korean** | 버팔로크러쉬 (Beopallo Keuleoswi) |
| **Affiliation** | Black Hook Crew / Edo Motors (later) |
| **Vehicle Mode** | Monster Truck/Pickup hybrid |
| **Metal Card** | Buffalo Plate |
| **Color** | Magenta |
| **Voice Actor (KR)** | Seo Won-seok (서원석) |

**Role**: Heavy hitter of the Black Hook Crew. Changed by Jun's mother's kindness.

**Background**: Friend of Black Hook and member of his pirate crew on Machina. Arrived on Earth later than Black Hook and sustained a landing injury causing fluid loss and extreme hunger. Despite being a villain, he was changed by Jun Grant's mother's kindness and eventually reformed.

**Personality**: Simple-minded rather than evil. Rough but honest, somewhat polite with a good heart. Very pure basic thinking with common sense slightly lacking. Obsessed with being "full-bellied." Power rivals Mega Trucker (the strongest among S1 Cardbots in raw power).

**Appearance**: Magenta Cardbot with Viking motif, green eyes, gold medal on forehead, dark-grey circle latch symbol on each side, large wheel on each shoulder, neon-green sections throughout on faceplate, shoulders, torso, legs and wheel rims.

**Quote**: *"Full-bellied Buffalo Crush! No one can beat me!"*

**Arsenal & Techniques**:
- **Buffalo Plate**: Buffalo head-framed translucent lime armor on chest
- **Buffalo Moonsault**: Main ultimate - jumps high and crushes opponent with rotating aura from plate (moonsault press posture)
- **Buffalo Vision**: Fires red laser beams from Buffalo Plate's eyes
- **Bone Chopper & Heart Lander**: Pair of axes for melee combat and green energy slashes
- **Tomahawk Crush**: Throws both axes boomerang-style

**Relationships**: Loyal crewmate of Black Hook. Friends with Buster Gallon. Changed by Jun's mother (Yuri).

**Story Notes**: Despite being a villain, one of the most sympathetic antagonists. His pure-heartedness makes him a fun character for stories.

### 11. Buster Gallon (버스터갤런) - The Genius Inventor

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Korean** | 버스터갤런 (Beoseuteo Gaelleon) |
| **Affiliation** | Black Hook Crew / Edo Motors (later) |
| **Vehicle Mode** | Bulldozer/Tractor hybrid |
| **Metal Card** | Buster Gatling |
| **Color** | Green / Teal |
| **Voice Actor (KR)** | Hwang Chang-young (황창영) |

**Role**: Genius inventor and strategist of the Black Hook Crew.

**Background**: Originally part of Black Hook's pirate crew. Went rogue after Buffalo Crush was sealed by Jun. Despite being outnumbered, he managed to incapacitate all Cardbots except Buffalo Crush on his own, proving his "genius inventor" title. Was eventually beaten by Buffalo Crush and joined Jun's group.

**Personality**: Smart, likes to fool others with pranks. Has a "mad scientist" personality. Cunning and strategic. Capable of building the Guardian Shield for Wild Guardy and other inventions.

**Appearance**: Green Cardbot with construction worker motif, hardhat/gas mask structured head with canister-shaped symbols on each side, green eyes, yellow triangle symbol and half a bulldozer blade on each forearm, white lower torso.

**Quote**: *"I, Buster Gallon, will gather the Metal Cardbots scattered throughout the universe and build a great empire in this galaxy!"*

**Arsenal & Techniques**:
- **Buster Gatling**: Translucent amber chest-mounted artillery (cannon on chest + gatling gun on each shoulder)
- **Buster Blaster**: Main ultimate - fires green laser beam from chest cannon
- **Twin Vulcan**: Fires energy beams from the gatling guns
- **Chest Cannon**: Generates and fires energy shells
- **Mini Gallons**: Small robot drones resembling Buster Gallon. Customizable with GPS, drills, scissors, propellers, and cameras

**Relationships**: Originally loyal to Black Hook. Went rogue but later reformed. Created the Guardian Shield for Wild Guardy.

**Story Notes**: Hilariously localized as "Busty Gallon" in English and Indonesian dubs.

### 12. Heavy Iron (헤비아이언) - The Tyrant

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Korean** | 헤비아이언 (Hebi Aieon) |
| **Affiliation** | Outlaw (later Edo Motors) |
| **Vehicle Mode** | 1970 Semi-Truck & Trailer |
| **Metal Card** | Iron Armor |
| **Color** | Silver / Crimson-Red / Indigo / Black |
| **Voice Actor (KR)** | Choi Han (최한) |

**Role**: Season 1 "Big Bad." The most powerful antagonist.

**Background**: The most infamous outlaw on Machina - even Deus Machina left him alone. Stole the Star Crown from Black Hook. His escape pod merged with space debris, arriving as a meteorite that nearly destroyed Mowtown. He couldn't transform until reuniting with his trailer. Plowed through 4 Cardbots simultaneously while handicapped. Eventually activated the Star Crown to terraform Earth into a new Machina. Defeated only by Ultimate Blue Cop (which required combining ALL other Cardbots). After being sealed, he slowly reintegrated but maintained a sour attitude.

**Personality**: Believes might makes right. Seeks to terraform Earth, disregarding human life. "Road Warrior" class - significantly larger and stronger than all other S1 Cardbots. After reform, still largely keeps to himself and butts heads with Black Hook.

**Appearance**: Multi-colored Cardbot (crimson-red/indigo/silver/black) with massive build. Black head with four crimson-red crest extensions, yellow eyes (sole Cardbot who can conceal pupils for menacing fully-yellow eyes look). Tallest Cardbot in Season 1. Trailer forms arms and legs; cab forms head and torso (similar to Optimus Prime transformation scheme).

**Quote**: *"Do you understand now, kids? I am the strongest Metal Cardbot, Heavy Iron!"*

**Arsenal & Techniques**:
- **Iron Armor**: Translucent indigo boomerang-shaped armor with red sections on torso
- **Iron Cannon**: Main ultimate - generates and fires powerful energy beam from Iron Armor
- **Heavy Buster Cannon**: Enhanced ultimate - destructive energy beam; even base state overpowers Season 2 final villain
- **Iron Sword**: Wrist blade with barrel on each arm
- **Iron Machine Gun**: Exhaust port guns on each shoulder
- **Star Crown** (former): Five-pronged Machinasium crown. Enhanced his powers, generated crystals, possessed terraforming properties. Destroyed by Blue Cop

**Relationships**: Feared by all Cardbots. Stole from Black Hook (ongoing rivalry). Eventually reforms but stays grumpy. Required ALL Cardbots combined to defeat.

**Story Notes**: Named because he "looked heavy :)". Design similar to Optimus Prime. Never received stock footage Set-Up sequence in Season 1 due to late joining.

## Ultimate Blue Cop - Combined Form

At the climax of Season 1, Blue Cop gains the **Ultimate Armor** by combining the power of ALL other Metal Cardbots (excluding Heavy Iron). This form grants:
- **Ultimate Armor**: Translucent gauntlets (red right, blue left) and boots (green right, purple left)
- Can use ALL teammates' techniques (silhouette of original owner appears when activated)
- **Lightning Overstrike**: Wraps lightning around both arm armors, strikes repeatedly, finishes with powerful punch
- **Infinite Punish**: Focuses all Ultimate Armor energy to fire four powerful beams

## Writing Tips for Metal Cardbot Stories

### Character Dynamics
- **Blue Cop + Jun**: Commander/partner dynamic. Jun commands strategy, Blue Cop executes
- **Mega Trucker**: Comic relief through dancing, but dependable in defense
- **Shadow X**: Childish pranks but deadly serious in battle
- **Wild Guardy + Crest**: Aristocratic duo that mirrors Jun + Blue Cop
- **Black Hook Trio**: Buffalo Crush is lovable despite being a villain; Buster Gallon is the schemer; Black Hook is the proud captain
- **Heavy Iron**: Reluctant ally, still a loner, frequently clashes with Black Hook

### Tone & Humor Sources
- Mega Trucker randomly breaking into dance during serious moments
- Shadow X's childish pranks using stealth mode
- Buffalo Crush's obsession with food and being "full-bellied"
- Buster Gallon's mad scientist gadgets (Mini Gallons)
- Phoenix Fire's lingering fire phobia
- Heavy Iron and Black Hook's constant bickering
- Edo's financial stress from powering all the Cardbots

### Battle Structure
1. Jun assesses the situation
2. Deploys a Cardbot by unsealing them from Metal Card
3. Slots the weapon Metal Card into Metal Breath to summon their weapon
4. Commands battlefield strategy
5. Ultimate technique finishes the fight
6. Defeated enemy Cardbot can be sealed with Metal Breath

### Key Themes
- **Coexistence**: Humans and alien refugees learning to live together
- **Friendship**: The Metal Breath's directive - "find a friend"
- **Redemption**: Every antagonist eventually joins the hero team
- **Empathy over rules**: Blue Cop learning justice requires heart, not just law
- **Protecting nature**: Dexter's arc about respecting Earth's environment
