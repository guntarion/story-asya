# CHARACTER GUIDE FOR KIDS STORY

## 1. SHINTA

**Basic Info:**

- Gender: Female
- Age: 12 years old
- Appearance: Embodies the sun with warm, golden glow around her

**Personality:**

- Bookworm who loves reading adventure stories and fairy tales
- Balances study time with play time perfectly
- Curious and always eager to learn new things
- Patient and kind, often helps younger kids with their questions
- Collector of colorful posters featuring her favorite book characters and inspiring quotes

**Special Traits:**

- Her room is like a library-museum with posters covering every wall
- Best friend with Starry despite their age difference
- Shares a love for the same video game character with Starry (probably a brave princess or magical hero)
- Glows brighter when excited about a new book or discovery
- Has a special reading corner where she likes to curl up with hot chocolate

**Role in Story:** The wise older sister figure who guides others with her knowledge and steady presence

---

## 2. METEORA

**Basic Info:**

- Gender: Male
- Age: 6 years old
- Appearance: Looks like a shooting star with a fiery tail that sparkles

**Personality:**

- Super extroverted and energetic
- Chatty and animated when comfortable with people
- Imaginative storyteller who loves to embellish tales
- Confident and adventurous
- Funny and entertaining

**Special Traits:**

- Pretends to be from America but actually from Indonesia (maybe picked it up from movies)
- Speaks with a mix of languages when excited
- His tail glows different colors when he tells stories
- Loves to zoom around quickly, leaving sparkly trails
- Sometimes his "American" accent slips when he's surprised

**Role in Story:** The energetic comic relief who brings laughter and excitement to every scene

---

## 3. INTAR

**Basic Info:**

- Gender: Male
- Age: 9 years old
- Appearance: Represents Earth with green and blue swirls, maybe small continents visible on his body

**Personality:**

- Honest and straightforward - always tells the truth
- Innocent and sees the good in everyone
- Optimistic and never complains
- Simple pleasures make him happy
- Loyal and dependable friend

**Special Traits:**

- Never uses negative words like "boring" - always finds something interesting
- Obsessed with foot-shaped lollipops (probably thinks they're hilarious)
- His colors change with seasons and weather
- Has a collection of different flavored foot lollipops
- Makes friends easily because of his genuine nature

**Role in Story:** The pure-hearted character who reminds everyone to find joy in simple things

---

## 4. GARFIELD

**Basic Info:**

- Gender: Male
- Age: 40 years old
- Appearance: Looks like a grandfather clock with moving hands and roman numerals

**Personality:**

- Serious and responsible father figure
- Bold and brave when protecting his family
- Caring and always puts his children first
- Helpful to everyone in the community
- Wise with good advice for any problem

**Special Traits:**

- His clock hands move faster when he's worried about his kids
- Makes gentle "tick-tock" sounds when thinking
- Always knows exactly what time it is anywhere in the world
- His chimes ring different melodies based on his mood
- Keeps a perfect schedule but always makes time for family

**Role in Story:** The protective father and community leader who provides stability and guidance

---

## 5. STARRY

**Basic Info:**

- Gender: Female
- Age: 3 years old
- Appearance: Adorable cat with shimmering stars on her ears, tail, and clothes

**Personality:**

- Moody little one whose emotions change quickly
- Hides her true feelings behind jokes and playfulness
- Sweet and lovable despite her mood swings
- Clever for her age with a good sense of humor
- Sensitive but tries to act tough

**Special Traits:**

- Her stars twinkle different colors based on her hidden emotions
- Makes funny faces and silly sounds when trying to cheer people up
- Sometimes her tail stars fall off when she's really upset
- Loves the same video game character as Shinta (creates special bond)
- Purrs when genuinely happy, which is rare but precious

**Role in Story:** The adorable youngest character whose emotional journey touches everyone's hearts

---

## 6. GRANT

**Basic Info:**

- Gender: Male
- Age: 6 years old
- Appearance: Fluffy hamster with soft brown fur and bright curious eyes

**Personality:**

- Very shy and takes time to warm up to new people
- Speaks in whispers or soft voices
- Gentle and kind-hearted
- Athletic despite his quiet nature
- Patient and observant

**Special Traits:**

- Carries a small ball everywhere he goes
- His fur fluffs up when nervous
- Amazing at sports but too shy to show off
- Squeaks softly when excited about something
- Stores snacks in his cheek pouches for sharing later

**Role in Story:** The quiet observer who surprises everyone with hidden talents and brave moments

---

## STORY RELATIONSHIPS:

- **Family:** Garfield is the caring father of Intar and Starry
- **Best Friends:** Shinta and Starry share a special bond despite age difference
- **Playmates:** Meteora and Grant are same age and could be great play companions
- **Community:** All characters live in the same magical neighborhood where celestial beings and earthly creatures coexist happily

This character guide provides rich material for adventures involving friendship, family bonds, growing up, and learning valuable life lessons perfect for children's stories!
